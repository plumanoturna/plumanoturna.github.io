What is Pluma Noturna?

Pluma Noturna is a content distribution platform specialized in relaxation and sleep media. It automates the publishing workflow across multiple social media platforms, delivering bedtime stories, ambient videos, and calming audio content to audiences worldwide.

The platform manages two channels — Pluma Noturna (Portuguese) and Night Feather Stories (English) — distributing content to millions of viewers seeking relaxation and better sleep.

How It Works

1

Content Creation

Original relaxation stories are produced with professional narration, ambient soundscapes, and calming visuals. Each story includes a full-length video, promotional clips, thumbnail artwork, and a podcast audio version.

2

Multi-Platform Publishing

The platform authenticates with each social media service via OAuth2 and publishes content using official APIs. Videos are uploaded to YouTube and TikTok, images and reels to Instagram and Pinterest, and audio episodes to Spotify via RSS.

3

Scheduling & Distribution

Content is scheduled for optimal delivery times across all platforms. Full videos, short clips, thumbnails, and podcast episodes are distributed on a coordinated schedule to maximize audience reach.

4

Audience Engagement

Viewers and listeners access relaxation content on their preferred platform. The platform ensures consistent content availability across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, and Spotify.

TikTok Integration

Pluma Noturna uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish relaxation videos directly to TikTok. The integration includes:

  • OAuth2 Authentication — Secure login via TikTok Login Kit
  • Direct Posting — Videos uploaded via Content Posting API (video.publish, video.upload)
  • Scheduled Publishing — Content distributed at coordinated times across all platforms
  • Multi-Channel Support — Both Pluma Noturna (PT) and Night Feather Stories (EN) channels
