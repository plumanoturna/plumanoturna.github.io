Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

About

This Privacy Policy applies to the Night Feather Stories application and the website www.plumanoturna.com.br/nightfeather. Night Feather Stories is a relaxing bedtime stories channel that uses automated publishing tools to distribute content across multiple platforms.

Data Collection

The Night Feather Stories application does not collect, store or process personal data from third parties. The application exclusively accesses the channel's own social media accounts for content uploading. No end-user data is collected or stored by the application.

Authentication

The Night Feather Stories application uses OAuth2 for authentication with third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest). Access tokens are stored locally on the channel administrator's device and are not shared with third parties.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies.

Third-Party Services

Night Feather Stories content is distributed through platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Pinterest, which have their own privacy policies. We recommend reading the privacy policies of these platforms.
